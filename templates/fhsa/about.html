About

A web application to make searching for possible illnesses, remedies, and articles relating to medical concerns simple.

Features

  • The ability to create a unique profile along with an auto-generated 'identijii' from your username.
  • Search three different APIs, with the ability to personalise results on what APIs to look at.
  • Results from each API are ranked on the sensitivity of the article - a calculation of the readability and sentimentality.
